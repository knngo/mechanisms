What is the Bar Raiser program?

The Bar Raiser program was created to balance the power of decision making to counterbalance a hiring manager's need to fill a position quickly. The Bar Raiser is an objective third party to the interview loop to assure the best long term hires are made for the company. This provides a balance of power in hiring decisions and a higher level of accountability to the High Hiring Bar. Together, the Bar Raiser, recruiter and hiring manager work hard to raise our recruiting standards in support of one of the most important company Leadership Principles: Hire and Develop the Best.

How does the Bar Raiser program mechanism work?

The Bar Raiser program addresses the business challenge of hiring at scale without diluting our high performance bar or peculiar culture. This mechanism transforms the set of inputs (candidates) into a set of desired outputs (Amazonians).
